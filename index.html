  Bio
Sanchit Misra is currently a research scientist at Intel’s Parallel Computing Lab, Bengaluru, India. His research interests include high-performance computational biology, machine learning, parallel algorithms, and application driven architecture research. He received a PhD degree in computer engineering from the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at Northwestern University in 2011.
